GlassLedger Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Overview

GlassLedger is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your financial data belongs entirely to you.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal or financial data. All information you enter — such as paychecks, bills, and totals — is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

  • Your data stays on your device.
  • We do not use external servers or cloud services.
  • Deleting the app permanently removes all stored information.

Third-Party Access

GlassLedger does not use analytics tools, ads, or third-party integrations that track user behavior or collect personal information.

Children’s Privacy

GlassLedger does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13, since no user data is collected or transmitted.

Your Control

Because all data is stored locally, you have full control at all times. You can delete your information simply by removing the app from your device.
