The maker behind the mark

Craft learned in the real world. Applied in code.

I’m James Shelby, an independent iOS developer from Sonoma County, California.

My path to software ran through emergency medical work, private security, and more than seven years in knife manufacturing. Each taught me the same lesson: details matter, dependable tools earn trust, and good work should have a clear purpose.

Dagger Development brings that mindset to software—focused products, thoughtful interfaces, and steady improvement with every release.
